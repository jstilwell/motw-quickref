Clothing: Denim jacket, black denim jeans.
Jewelry: Bead necklace, lots of bracelets.

BACKSTORY

Eliza, half Native American, was born in Arizona and abandoned shortly thereafter. Her mother was white and her father was Navajo and Eliza was the result of a one night stand. She was adopted by a loving white family and then moved to a tiny coastal town in Massachusetts.

She had a mostly uneventful childhood. She was taught to hunt and handle guns by her father. She did well in school and played basketball for the middle school and high school teams. She showed aptitude for math early on. It eventually led to her attending the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

Having never had alcohol or drugs, she was talked into taking magic mushrooms in her freshman year. During her first experience, Eliza believes that she was recruited and trained by a race of aliens tasked with protecting the earth from supernatural interdemensional monsters.

Well on her way to becoming a professional programmer, she found the hallucinagenic experience overwhelming and dropped out of college soon after. She looked for any and all hallucinagens to try to talk to the aliens again, but she could never make contact with them again.

In the meantime, she took the aliens seriously and when she wasn't tripping, Eliza dedicated all of her time to studying the supernatural and the occult. She used her computer skills to create a database where she kept all of her findings -- the first of its kind in digital form.
